Unity Showcase

A technical demo showcasing Unity's AI system and physics engine

View Project

A simple sheep herding game where the player controls a dog around a farm. The player must locate sheep and herd them back to the enclosure. The player can then shear any sheep to earn money allowing them to buy more sheep and expand the farm.

I wanted to challenge myself and learn Unity's AI pathfinding, the sheep are able to navigate their way around the different terrain, buildings and fencing while also keeping their distance from the sheep dog.

I utilise Unity's physics engine to allow the player to locate and react to the environment and sheep. The dog the player controls is able to find the closest sheep first and travel to it across the environment then it is up to the player to drive the sheep back to the enclosure.

Using high definition external assets I have created an interactive escape the room demo in a 3D environment. The demo features three near identical rooms each connected to one another with small but noticable differences the player must spot.

All three rooms have a escape door, but only one is the true escape. The player must navigate through the rooms finding meaning in mundane objects to find the real room to escape from.
